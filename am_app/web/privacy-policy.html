This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, share, and protect information when you use our website, web application, and mobile application (collectively, the “Services”). By creating an account or using the Services, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

Summary
  • We collect account and usage information needed to provide portfolio, trading, market, and related investment tools.
  • We store authentication tokens securely on your device.
  • Some features (such as Google Sign-In or optional Gmail sync) involve third-party services you choose to use.
  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • You may contact us to access, correct, or delete your account data, subject to legal and operational requirements.

1. Who we are

The Services are provided by AM Portfolio, operating under the Asrax brand. For privacy-related requests, contact us at admin@asrax.in.

2. Information we collect

Depending on how you use the Services, we may collect the following categories of information:

2.1 Account and identity information

  • Email address, user ID, display name, and profile photo (if provided)
  • Authentication credentials (passwords are processed by our identity systems; we do not store plain-text passwords in the app)
  • Information received when you sign in with Google, such as your Google account email and basic profile details, in accordance with your Google account permissions

2.2 Financial and investment-related information

  • Portfolio names and identifiers, holdings, transactions, trade journal entries, metrics, templates, and related analytics you create or import
  • Broker or portfolio data you choose to connect or upload, including through optional integrations
  • Documents and files you upload for processing (for example, statements or reports in document intelligence features)

2.3 Optional integrations

  • If you connect Gmail or similar services for portfolio sync, we process the information required to perform that sync according to the permissions you grant. You can disconnect these integrations at any time.

2.4 Subscription and billing information

  • Subscription plan, billing interval, and payment status
  • Payment details are handled by our payment/subscription processors where applicable; we do not store full payment card numbers on our application servers

2.5 Device, app, and usage information

  • Device type, operating system, app version, browser type, and general diagnostic or performance data
  • Session and navigation preferences stored locally to restore your last view (for example, selected module, portfolio, or tab)
  • Authentication tokens stored in secure device storage to keep you signed in
  • Log and telemetry data related to app startup, errors, API connectivity, and service reliability

2.6 Communications

  • Messages you send to us for support, account recovery, email verification, or password reset

3. How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Create and manage your account and authenticate you
  • Provide core features such as dashboard, portfolio tracking, trade management, market data, document intelligence, and subscriptions
  • Sync and display your investment data across devices when you are signed in
  • Process optional integrations you enable (such as Google Sign-In or Gmail sync)
  • Improve performance, security, debugging, and product reliability
  • Communicate with you about account activity, security, service updates, and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms

4. Legal bases (where applicable)

Where required by law, we process personal information based on one or more of the following: performance of a contract (providing the Services), your consent (for optional integrations), legitimate interests (security, analytics, product improvement), and legal compliance.

5. How we share information

We may share information only as described below:

  • Service providers: Hosting, cloud infrastructure, authentication, analytics, payment processing, email delivery, and support vendors that help us operate the Services under contractual confidentiality and security obligations
  • Third-party sign-in providers: Google Sign-In, when you choose to authenticate with Google
  • Integrations you authorize: For example, Gmail or broker-related services you connect
  • Legal and safety: When required by law, regulation, court order, or to protect rights, safety, and security
  • Business transfers: In connection with a merger, acquisition, financing, or sale of assets, subject to appropriate safeguards

We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data storage and security

We use administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect your information, including encrypted storage for sensitive tokens on supported devices, secure API communication, and access controls on our systems. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Data may be stored on your device (for example, session preferences and secure authentication tokens) and on servers operated by us or our service providers.

7. Data retention

We retain personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations. When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it in accordance with our retention practices, unless law requires longer retention.

8. Your choices and rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access, update, or correct your account information through profile/settings features
  • Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Withdraw consent for optional integrations (such as Gmail sync or Google Sign-In alternatives)
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, where applicable by law
  • Request a copy of your personal information in a portable format, where applicable

To exercise these rights, email admin@asrax.in. We may need to verify your identity before responding.

9. Account deletion

You may request account deletion by contacting admin@asrax.in. After verification, we will delete or anonymize personal data unless retention is required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or backup purposes.

10. Children’s privacy

The Services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

11. International transfers
